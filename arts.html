Arts throughout the years have depicted the culture and history of mankind thus leading to the origin of arts dating back to the beginning of humanity resulting into Arts translating tradition by depicting them in forms of literature, music, dance, painting, sculpture, movies and photography. Entrance Exams for Arts

To get enrolled in an arts graduate degree most colleges consider 12th standard marks percentage only, while some universities have their specific entrance exams. Specific exams are:

  • Jawaharlal Nehru University Entrance Exam (JNUEE)
    - It is an online exam conducted by the National Testing Agency for undergraduates and graduates. The courses offered are all around the arts. Around 1 lakh aspirants appear for this test every year.
  • Banaras Hindu University Undergraduate Entrance Test (BHU UET)
    - Another entrance test conducted every year for undergraduates and graduates of arts is BHU UET. It is conducted both online and offline. They test you on mental ability, numerical ability, general knowledge and language comprehension.
  • Delhi University Entrance Test (DUET) - The National Testing Agency organises this exam on behalf of Delhi University. This exam is conducted each year for two hours and is conducted online. They test students on current affairs, logical reasoning, and analytical ability and communication skills.
  • Tata Institute of Social Science Bachelor's Admission Test (TISS NET) - This is a computer-based entrance exam conducted once a year. It tests on Mathematics, English and General Awareness. Around 1 lakh students apply for this exam.
